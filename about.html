About Us

ISLANDS is an organization that deals with the publication of some basic information about different islands around the world to inform people with natural beauties.

Since 2010 we accept new people in our organization who bring us new ideas and contribute with their general knowledge of different islands whether in geographical, political or even cultural terms.With some of them we also organize weekly camping to visit an island in order to get to know the place better and the things that identify it.

What We Do

We try to be in touch with the population for any new information about the newest islands discovered in the world in order to develop tourism there and grow the economy.
